Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Kolkata, The Bengal Bite was born from a passion for preserving the timeless recipes passed down through generations. We believe in the magic of 'gorom-gorom' (piping hot) food, the warmth of hospitality, and the joy of a shared meal. Our chefs use traditional techniques and locally-sourced ingredients to bring you a truly authentic and unforgettable dining experience.
